7.Service limitations

Home Call Guard uses automated call-screening technology, including keyword analysis and AI classification, to help identify likely scam or nuisance calls. This significantly reduces your exposure to unwanted calls, but it is not a guarantee. No automated system can identify every scam or nuisance call with complete accuracy, and some genuine scam calls may not be detected, while some genuine calls may occasionally be flagged incorrectly. You should continue to apply your own judgement to any call, and never share financial or personal information with a caller you do not trust, regardless of how the call was screened.

AI screening limitations

Our AI classification step works from an automated, real-time transcription of what a caller says, and from keyword analysis. This means its accuracy can be affected by things like background noise, unclear speech, strong accents, non-English speech, or a scam caller deliberately avoiding language our system recognises as suspicious. Scam techniques also change over time, and our detection methods may not immediately recognise new or unusual approaches. AI classification is a tool to reduce risk, not a substitute for your own caution.
